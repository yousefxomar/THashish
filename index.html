    About Us

    Established in 1994 by Mr. Taha Hashish, our firm has swiftly risen to prominence as a leading public accountants and consultants firm in Egypt. Specializing in accounting, auditing, and tax services, we cater to a diverse clientele both locally and internationally. Over the years, we've expanded our offerings to include comprehensive services such as economic feasibility studies, financial consultancy, legal advisory, and project financing evaluation. With a highly qualified team of partners, consultants, and managers, we are committed to delivering top-notch services across various sectors, ensuring our clients receive tailored solutions that drive success.

    Mr. Taha Hashish
    Before taking such a step and founding our chartered accountants office in 1994, Taha Hashish accumulated invaluable experience in various accounting and financial roles, including several years at the Central Bank of Egypt. This diverse background equipped him with the expertise and confidence necessary to take such a leap, and now his office has 60+ employees. As our esteemed founder and main partner, he epitomizes excellence in the realms of accounting and consultancy. His distinguished background, highlighted by accreditations from the prestigious Accountants & Auditors Register and a Tax & Auditing Diploma, is complemented by his status as a Fellow of the Egyptian Tax Association. Moreover, his roles as an authorized office holder within the Egyptian Accountants & Auditors Association and the Central Authority for Accountancy underscore his dedication to upholding industry standards. With registrations with regulatory bodies such as the Central Bank of Egypt, Taha Hashish's profound impact within the financial sector is undeniable. Under his visionary leadership, our firm continues to set unparalleled benchmarks for professionalism, integrity, and client satisfaction.
    Mr. Tarek Hashish
    Tarek Hashish serves as a cornerstone of our firm as Main Partner and CEO, bringing deep expertise in accounting and auditing. Listed on the Accountants & Auditors Register, he is committed to upholding high professional standards and ethics. Under his leadership, the business has grown to more than four times its original size, a testament to his strategic vision and effective direction. Beyond his contributions within the firm, Tarek is a Fellow of the Egyptian Tax Association and actively participates in the Egyptian Accountants & Auditors Association, the Egyptian Society of Accountants & Auditors, and the Accountability State Authority. His engagement in these organizations ensures he remains well-informed on new laws and tax updates, keeping the firm aligned with the latest industry developments.
